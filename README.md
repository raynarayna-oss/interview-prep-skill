# 🎯 Interview Prep Skill for Claude

> A Claude skill that turns interview anxiety into interview confidence — tailored to every job you apply for.

**English** | [中文版](#中文版)

---

## What Is This?

This is a **Claude skill** — an installable add-on that supercharges Claude with specialized interview preparation capabilities. Once installed, Claude becomes your personal interview coach, helping you prepare for every stage of the hiring process across multiple applications simultaneously.

Optimized for **Marketing, Brand, and Growth roles**, but designed to work across any industry.

---

## What Can It Do?

The skill includes **11 preparation modules** that cover the full interview journey:

| Module | What It Helps With |
|--------|-------------------|
| 🎤 Self-Introduction | Write a tailored "tell me about yourself" for each company and role |
| 💬 Core Behavioral Questions | Prep the 3 Whys, P&G's 8 key questions, and STAR method answers |
| 🔮 Question Prediction | Predict what interviewers will ask based on the job description, with sample answers |
| 📄 Resume Deep-Dives | Turn resume bullets into rich STAR stories with metrics and impact |
| 🤝 High-EQ Scenarios | Handle tricky interpersonal situations (difficult colleagues, heavy workload, conflicts) with grace |
| 🧠 Values & Personality | Answer culture-fit, weakness, and motivation questions authentically |
| 🧵 Career Narrative | Build a cohesive story that connects all your past experiences to the new role |
| 🕳️ Weakness & Gap Handling | Reframe career gaps, pivots, or missing qualifications confidently |
| 📊 Case Interview Prep | Tackle go-to-market, growth diagnosis, and campaign brief scenarios |
| ❓ Questions to Ask | Prepare thoughtful, role-specific questions for your interviewers |
| 🔍 Company Research | Get a pre-interview briefing on the company's business, news, and culture |

---

## How to Install

1. Download the `interview-prep.skill` file from this repository
2. Open [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai)
3. Go to **Settings** → **Skills** → **Install Skill**
4. Upload the `.skill` file
5. Start a new conversation — Claude will now have interview prep capabilities!

---

## How to Use

Just tell Claude what you need. For example:

- *"Help me prep for an interview at [Company] for a [Role] position. Here's the JD: ..."*
- *"Write me a self-introduction for this marketing manager role."*
- *"What questions will they likely ask me, and how should I answer them?"*
- *"Help me turn this resume bullet into a STAR story."*
- *"How do I answer 'what's your weakness?' for a brand strategy role?"*

You can paste your resume, job description, or even interview experiences you've found online, and Claude will tailor everything to your specific situation.

---

## Who Is This For?

- Job seekers applying to **multiple companies simultaneously**
- Candidates targeting **Marketing, Brand, Growth, or Strategy** roles
- Anyone who wants **structured, personalized** interview coaching — not generic advice
- People preparing for **MNC structured interviews** (P&G, Unilever, L'Oréal, etc.) or startup interviews

# 中文版

# 🎯 Claude 面试备考技能包

> 让每一场面试都有针对性地准备——从自我介绍到棘手问题，全程覆盖。

---

## 这是什么？

这是一个 **Claude 技能包（Skill）**——安装后，Claude 将具备专业的面试辅导能力，成为你的私人面试教练。无论你同时在投递多少家公司，它都能帮你针对每个职位量身定制备考内容。

本技能包针对**市场营销、品牌、增长类职位**深度优化，同时适用于其他行业。

---

## 它能做什么？

技能包包含 **11 个备考模块**，覆盖面试全流程：

| 模块 | 功能 |
|------|------|
| 🎤 自我介绍 | 针对不同公司和职位，撰写个性化的"请做个自我介绍" |
| 💬 核心行为面试题 | 准备"三个为什么"、宝洁八大问、STAR 法则答案 |
| 🔮 面试题预测 | 根据 JD 预测面试官可能提问，并提供示范答案 |
| 📄 简历经历深挖 | 将简历条目扩展为有数据支撑的 STAR 故事 |
| 🤝 高情商应对 | 处理职场人际难题（跨部门不配合、多头管理、冲突等） |
| 🧠 价值观与性格题 | 真实、有力地回答文化契合度、弱点、动机类问题 |
| 🧵 职业故事线 | 梳理贯穿所有经历的叙事逻辑，让跳槽和转行显得合理 |
| 🕳️ 空窗期与转型 | 自信应对简历空白、职能转型、不符合要求的质疑 |
| 📊 案例面试备考 | 应对市场进入、增长诊断、活动策划等情景题 |
| ❓ 向面试官提问 | 准备有深度、有针对性的提问，留下好印象 |
| 🔍 公司信息速查 | 面试前快速了解公司业务、近期动态与文化信号 |

---

## 如何安装

1. 从本仓库下载 `interview-prep.skill` 文件
2. 打开 [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai)
3. 进入 **Settings（设置）** → **Skills（技能）** → **Install Skill（安装技能）**
4. 上传 `.skill` 文件
5. 开启新对话——Claude 现在已具备面试辅导能力！

---

## 如何使用

直接告诉 Claude 你需要什么，例如：

- *"我要面试【公司名】的【职位名】，帮我准备一下。这是 JD：……"*
- *"帮我为这个市场经理职位写一段自我介绍。"*
- *"他们可能会问我哪些问题？我该怎么回答？"*
- *"帮我把这条简历经历用 STAR 法则展开讲。"*
- *"品牌策略岗的面试，'你的缺点是什么'怎么回答比较好？"*

你可以直接粘贴简历、职位描述，甚至网上找到的面经，Claude 会根据你的实际情况量身定制所有内容。

---

## 适合哪些人？

- 同时投递**多家公司**、需要高效备考的求职者
- 应聘**市场营销、品牌、增长、商业策略**类职位的候选人
- 希望获得**结构化、个性化**面试指导（而非通用建议）的求职者
- 备战**外资结构化面试**（宝洁、联合利华、欧莱雅等）或互联网公司面试的候选人
